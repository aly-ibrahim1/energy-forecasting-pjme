# Energy Consumption Forecasting — Project Plan

**Dataset:** UCI Household Electric Power Consumption (2006–2010).  
**Target:** Global_active_power aggregated to daily mean.  
**Forecast horizon:** 7 days ahead.  
**Baselines:** Naïve (last value), Seasonal Naïve (7-day lag).  
**Metrics:** MAE, RMSE, MAPE.
